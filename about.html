About Me

Hi! I’m Dennis Infinity, a passionate web developer and designer from Kenya. I love creating clean, modern websites that not only look great but also provide a smooth user experience.

Over the past few years, I’ve worked on various projects, from personal portfolios to small business websites. My goal is to turn ideas into functional, visually appealing online experiences.

I’m skilled in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and responsive design. I enjoy experimenting with new tools and techniques to make my projects stand out.

When I’m not coding, I spend my time exploring UI/UX trends, learning new technologies, and sometimes creating content for my blog to share what I’ve learned with the community.

Feel free to browse my portfolio to see some of my work, and don’t hesitate to get in touch if you’d like to collaborate!
